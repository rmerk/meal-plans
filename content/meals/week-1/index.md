# Week 1 Overview

This week focuses on foundational batch cooking techniques that save time and money. You'll learn how to prep proteins, chop vegetables efficiently, and cook grains in bulk for the entire week.

## What You'll Learn

- Basic batch cooking workflow
- Protein preparation and storage
- Efficient vegetable chopping techniques
- Grain cooking in large quantities
- Proper food storage methods

## Recipes Included

This week includes 6 delicious recipes that utilize your batch-cooked ingredients:

1. Chicken Stir-Fry with Vegetables
2. Tofu Buddha Bowl
3. Egg Fried Rice
4. Chicken and Quinoa Bowl
5. Vegetable Tofu Scramble
6. Asian-Inspired Chicken Salad

Each recipe is designed to be quick to assemble since you've already done the batch cooking prep work!
