# Chicken Stir-Fry with Vegetables

A delicious and healthy chicken stir-fry loaded with colorful vegetables.

## Instructions

1. Heat sesame oil in a large wok or skillet over medium-high heat
2. Add chicken and cook until golden brown, about 6-8 minutes
3. Add garlic and ginger, stir for 30 seconds until fragrant
4. Add broccoli and bell peppers, stir-fry for 5 minutes
5. Pour in soy sauce and toss everything together
6. Cook for 2-3 more minutes until vegetables are tender-crisp
7. Serve hot over rice or noodles
