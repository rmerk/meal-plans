# Meal Prep Power Bowls

Perfect make-ahead lunch bowls packed with protein and nutrients.

## Instructions

1. Cook quinoa according to package directions and let cool
2. Season chicken breast with salt, pepper, and herbs
3. Grill chicken until fully cooked, about 6-7 minutes per side
4. Slice chicken into strips
5. Divide quinoa among 6 containers
6. Top each with chicken, greens, tomatoes, cucumber, avocado, and feta
7. Store in refrigerator for up to 4 days
8. Add dressing when ready to eat
