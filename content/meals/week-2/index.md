# Week 2 Overview

This week is all about one-pot meals that minimize cleanup while delivering maximum flavor. Perfect for busy weeknights when you don't want to spend hours washing dishes!

## What You'll Learn

- One-pot cooking techniques
- Layering flavors in a single pot
- Timing ingredients for perfect doneness
- Using beans for protein and fiber
- Cleanup-saving strategies

## Recipes Included

This week includes 6 hearty one-pot meals:

1. Beef and Bean Chili
2. Sausage and White Bean Soup
3. One-Pot Pasta Bolognese
4. Black Bean and Rice Skillet
5. Italian Sausage and Vegetable Stew
6. Beef and Barley Soup

All recipes designed for minimal dishes and maximum satisfaction!
