# Beef Tacos with Fresh Salsa

Classic beef tacos with all the fixings.

## Instructions

1. Brown ground beef in a large skillet over medium-high heat
2. Drain excess fat
3. Add taco seasoning and 1/4 cup water, simmer for 5 minutes
4. Warm taco shells according to package directions
5. Fill shells with seasoned beef
6. Top with lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, and sour cream
7. Serve immediately with lime wedges
