# Week 3 Overview

This week explores the wonderful world of sheet pan cooking. Everything cooks together on one pan in the oven, giving you hands-off time while dinner prepares itself!

## What You'll Learn

- Sheet pan cooking principles
- Proper vegetable cutting for even roasting
- Marinating techniques for maximum flavor
- Timing vegetables and proteins together
- Temperature control for perfect results

## Recipes Included

This week includes 6 delicious sheet pan dinners:

1. Lemon Herb Salmon with Asparagus
2. Mediterranean Chicken Thighs and Vegetables
3. Spicy Roasted Chickpeas and Sweet Potatoes
4. Teriyaki Salmon with Broccoli
5. Honey Mustard Chicken with Root Vegetables
6. Roasted Chickpea and Veggie Bowls

Set it, forget it, and enjoy perfectly roasted dinners with minimal cleanup!
