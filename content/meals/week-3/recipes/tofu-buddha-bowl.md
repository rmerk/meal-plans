# Crispy Tofu Buddha Bowl

A nourishing plant-based bowl with crispy tofu and vibrant vegetables.

## Instructions

1. Press tofu to remove excess moisture, cut into cubes
2. Toss tofu with cornstarch, salt, and pepper
3. Bake at 400¬∞F for 25 minutes, flipping halfway
4. Massage kale with a pinch of salt until softened
5. Assemble bowls with rice, kale, carrots, edamame, and crispy tofu
6. Drizzle with tahini dressing
7. Sprinkle with sesame seeds
8. Serve warm or at room temperature
