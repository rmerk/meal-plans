Morning Strategy

Unlike your lunches and dinners, breakfast does not need to be batch prepped. These recipes are designed to be made fresh in under 10 minutes.

  • ✓ Goal: 30-40g of protein to start the day.
  • ✓ Rotation: Pick 1-2 favorites and rotate them to keep shopping simple.
  • ✓ Speed: All recipes use fast-cooking ingredients (eggs, tofu, yogurt).

The Recipes

1. Savory Kimchi & Egg Scramble

High Protein

A savory, probiotic-packed way to start the day.

Ingredients

  • 3 whole eggs (whisked)
  • 1/2 cup kimchi, chopped
  • 1 green onion, sliced
  • 1/2 tsp sesame oil
  • 1/2 cup cooked rice (warm)
  • Optional: Gochugaru, Furikake

Instructions

  1. Sauté white parts of onion and kimchi in a pan for 2 mins.
  2. Add eggs and scramble gently until 80% cooked.
  3. Turn off heat. Stir in sesame oil and green onion tops.
  4. Serve over warm rice. Top with furikake.

2. Mango-Ginger Protein Yogurt

Fastest (2 Mins)

Tastes like dessert, fuels like a meal. Can be made the night before.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup (220g) Non-Fat Greek Yogurt
  • 1 scoop Vanilla Whey Protein
  • 1/2 cup mango chunks (frozen/fresh)
  • 1 tsp fresh grated ginger
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds

Instructions

  1. Mix yogurt and protein powder vigorously until smooth.
  2. Stir in ginger and chia seeds.
  3. Top with mango chunks.
  4. Tip: Let sit for 10 mins or overnight for thicker texture.

3. Spicy Tofu & Avocado Scramble

Plant Based

Hearty and savory. Great for days you want a break from eggs.

Ingredients

  • 7 oz (1/2 block) extra-firm tofu
  • 1/2 avocado, sliced
  • 1/2 cup mushrooms, diced
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric (for color)
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • Sriracha to taste

Instructions

  1. Crumble tofu with hands into a bowl.
  2. Sauté mushrooms in a pan until browned.
  3. Add tofu and turmeric. Fry for 3-4 mins.
  4. Add soy sauce, cook 1 min.
  5. Serve topped with avocado and sriracha.
