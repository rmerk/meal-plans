Detailed Recipes

1. Gochugaru Beef & Broccoli Bowl

Lunch (4 Servings)

Ingredients

  • 1.25 lbs 93/7 lean ground beef
  • 8 cups broccoli florets
  • 4 cups cooked brown rice
  • Sauce:
  • 6 tbsp soy sauce
  • 4-8 tsp gochugaru (to taste)
  • 4 tsp honey, 4 tsp sesame oil
  • 8 cloves garlic, 4 tsp ginger

Instructions

  1. Steam broccoli until tender-crisp.
  2. Whisk sauce ingredients together.
  3. Brown beef in a large skillet; drain fat.
  4. Add sauce to beef, thicken for 2 mins.
  5. Toss in broccoli to coat.
  6. Portion over rice. Garnish with green onions.

2. Vietnamese-Style Chicken Salad

Lunch (3 Servings)

Ingredients

  • 1.125 lbs chicken breast (cooked/shredded)
  • 6 cups cabbage, 3 cups carrots
  • 1.5 cups cucumber, cilantro, mint
  • Dressing:
  • Juice of 3 limes, 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 tsp sesame oil, 3 tsp honey
  • 3 tsp soy sauce, 3 cloves garlic

Instructions (Deconstructed)

  1. Combine cabbage, carrots, herbs for base.
  2. Whisk dressing ingredients.
  3. Storage: Keep salad base, chicken, and dressing in 3 separate containers per meal.
  4. Mix just before eating to keep crisp.

3. Classic Beef & Broccoli Stir-Fry

Dinner (4 Servings)

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs flank steak (thinly sliced)
  • 12 cups broccoli, 4 onions
  • 4 tsp cornstarch + 4 tbsp soy sauce (marinade)
  • Sauce:
  • 1/4 cup broth, 8 tbsp soy sauce
  • 4 tsp sesame oil, 4 tsp honey
  • 8 cloves garlic, 4 tsp ginger

Instructions

  1. Marinate beef in cornstarch/soy (10 min).
  2. Sear beef in batches on high heat. Remove.
  3. Stir-fry veggies. Add water to steam (2 min).
  4. Return beef and add sauce. Cook until thick.
  5. Serve over brown rice.

4. Pork & Soba Noodle Stir-Fry

Dinner (3 Servings)

Ingredients

  • 1.125 lbs pork loin (thinly sliced)
  • 6 oz dry soba noodles
  • 12-15 baby bok choy, 3 peppers
  • Sauce:
  • 9 tbsp soy sauce, 3 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 3 tsp sesame oil, 3 tsp honey
  • 6 cloves garlic, 3 tsp ginger

Instructions

  1. Cook soba noodles, rinse cold.
  2. Stir-fry pork until cooked. Remove.
  3. Stir-fry veggies (bok choy/peppers).
  4. Add pork, noodles, and sauce back in.
  5. Toss until hot and combined.
