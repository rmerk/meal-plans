Shopping Tips

Pre-Prepped Vegetables

Consider buying pre-shredded cabbage and carrots to save 15-20 minutes of prep time. Perfect for the Vietnamese chicken salad!

Budget-Friendly Swaps

Flank steak can be swapped for sirloin or even chuck steak if you slice it thin and marinate well. Ground turkey works great instead of ground beef for a leaner option.

Gochugaru Substitute

If you can't find gochugaru (Korean chili flakes), use crushed red pepper flakes with a pinch of paprika for color. Start with half the amount as it's spicier!
