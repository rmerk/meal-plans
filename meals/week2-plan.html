Order of Operations

  1. 1. Prep Carbs

    Cook **7 cups of dry brown rice**. This is your base for all 14 meals. Let it cool.

  2. 2. Prep Tofu

    Press, cube, and toss tofu with cornstarch. Pan-fry, bake, or air-fry until crispy. Set aside.

  3. 3. Veggies & Aromatics

    Mince all garlic/ginger. Slice all green onions, peppers, etc. Blanch all green beans.

  4. 4. Mix Sauces

    Make all 4 sauces in separate, labeled jars so they are ready to go.

  5. 5. Cook & Assemble

    Work one recipe at a time. Cook the protein, add veggies and sauce. Portion over 1 cup of rice per container.

  6. 6. Cool & Store

    Let all hot meals cool 30-45 mins before lidding. Stack and refrigerate.

Detailed Recipes

1. Spicy Turkey "Bibimbap" Bowl

Lunch (4 Servings)

Ingredients

  • 1.25 lbs ground turkey
  • 4 cups cooked brown rice
  • 6 oz fresh spinach
  • 2 cups shredded carrots
  • 2 cups bean sprouts
  • 4 eggs (for serving)
  • Turkey Sauce:
  • 3 tbsp gochujang, 2 tbsp soy
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil, 1 tbsp honey

Instructions

  1. Cook turkey, drain fat. Add sauce, simmer 3-4 min.
  2. Wipe pan. Sauté spinach with garlic/soy. Remove.
  3. Sauté carrots/sprouts with garlic/soy.
  4. Portion: 1 cup rice, 1/4 turkey, 1/4 spinach, 1/4 carrots/sprouts.
  5. Top with a fresh fried egg when you reheat.

2. Ginger-Garlic Ground Chicken

Lunch (3 Servings)

Ingredients

  • 1.125 lbs ground chicken
  • 3 cups cooked brown rice
  • 1/4 cup minced ginger
  • 6 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 green onions, sliced
  • 1 can water chestnuts, chopped
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce, 1 tbsp rice vinegar

Instructions

  1. Sauté ginger, garlic, and green onion whites (1-2 min).
  2. Add ground chicken and cook through.
  3. Stir in soy sauce, vinegar, water chestnuts, and half of the green onion tops. Cook 2 min.
  4. Portion over rice. Garnish with fresh greens.

3. Spicy-Sweet Crispy Tofu

Dinner (4 Servings)

Ingredients

  • 28 oz extra-firm tofu (pressed)
  • 2 tbsp cornstarch
  • 2 bell peppers, 1 large onion
  • 1.5 cups snap peas
  • 4 cups cooked brown rice
  • Sauce:
  • 1/3 cup soy, 3 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp sriracha, 1 tbsp rice vinegar

Instructions

  1. Cube tofu, toss with cornstarch. Pan-fry until crispy. Set aside.
  2. Stir-fry peppers and onion (4-5 min).
  3. Add snap peas, cook 2 min.
  4. Add tofu and sauce. Toss 1-2 min.
  5. Portion over rice.

4. Ground Pork & Green Bean

Dinner (3 Servings)

Ingredients

  • 1.125 lbs ground pork
  • 1.5 lbs green beans, trimmed
  • 3 cups cooked brown rice
  • 4 cloves garlic, 2 tsp ginger
  • Sauce:
  • 3 tbsp soy, 2 tbsp dark soy
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil, 1 tsp honey

Instructions

  1. Blanch green beans 3-4 min. Rinse cold.
  2. Brown pork in a hot wok.
  3. Add garlic and ginger, cook 1 min.
  4. Add beans and sauce. Toss 2-3 min.
  5. Portion over rice.
