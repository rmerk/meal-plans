Week 3: High-Protein Breakfasts

Week 3 Strategy

New week, new flavors. These recipes introduce savory oats and a smoothie to give you high-protein options beyond eggs and yogurt.

  • ✓ Variety: Three new flavors to prevent boredom.
  • ✓ Rotation: Pick 1-2 favorites for the week to simplify shopping.
  • ✓ Speed: The smoothie is the fastest option (2 min). The scramble and oats are < 10 min.

The Recipes

1. Savory Oatmeal with Fried Egg

Hearty & Warm

A fast, hearty, and fiber-packed way to start the day. Very filling.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup old-fashioned oats
  • 1 cup low-sodium broth
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 green onion, sliced
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp chili crisp (optional)

Instructions

  1. Bring broth to a boil, add oats, simmer 5 min.
  2. While oats cook, fry an egg in a separate pan.
  3. Pour oats into a bowl.
  4. Top with fried egg, green onion, soy, & chili crisp.

2. Spinach & Berry Protein Smoothie

Fastest (2 Mins)

The fastest way to get 40g+ of protein and a full serving of greens.

Ingredients

  • 1 scoop Vanilla/Unflavored Whey
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 cup fresh spinach
  • 1/2 cup frozen berries
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds
  • 1 tbsp almond butter

Instructions

  1. Add all ingredients to a blender.
  2. Blend on high for 45-60 seconds.
  3. Add a splash of water if too thick.

3. Pesto Spinach Egg Scramble

5-Min Prep

Uses pre-made pesto for a huge flavor boost. Serve with avocado.

Ingredients

  • 3 whole eggs
  • 1 cup fresh spinach
  • 1 tbsp basil pesto (jar)
  • 1/2 avocado (on the side)
  • Optional: 2 tbsp feta

Instructions

  1. Wilt spinach in a hot pan (1 min).
  2. Add whisked eggs. Scramble until 80% done.
  3. Turn off heat. Stir in pesto (and feta).
  4. Serve immediately with sliced avocado.
