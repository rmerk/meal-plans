Order of Operations

  1. 1. Prep Carbs

    Cook **7 cups of dry brown rice**. This is your base for all 14 meals. Let it cool.

  2. 2. Veggies & Aromatics

    Chop everything. Mince all garlic/ginger. Slice peppers, onions, mushrooms. Trim asparagus. Wash/halve bok choy.

  3. 3. Prep Proteins

    Cut chicken thighs. Press/cube extra-firm tofu. Drain/cube soft tofu.

  4. 4. Mix Sauces

    Make the sauces for Mapo, Teriyaki, and Ginger Tofu in 3 separate jars.

  5. 5. Cook & Assemble

    Work one recipe at a time. The Green Curry needs a large pot. The other 3 can be made in a wok/skillet.

  6. 6. Cool & Store

    Let all 14 hot meals cool 30-45 mins before lidding.

Detailed Recipes

1. Thai Green Curry with Chicken

Lunch (4 Servings)

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs chicken thighs, cubed
  • 2 bell peppers, 1 large onion
  • 1.5 cups snap peas
  • 4 tbsp green curry paste
  • 2 cans Light Coconut Milk
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce, 1 lime
  • 4 cups cooked brown rice

Instructions

  1. Sauté peppers/onions. Add curry paste, cook 1 min.
  2. Add chicken, brown slightly.
  3. Add coconut milk and soy. Simmer 15-20 min.
  4. Stir in snap peas and lime juice. Cook 2 min.
  5. Portion over 1 cup rice each.

2. Szechuan-Style "Mapo" Beef

Lunch (3 Servings)

Ingredients

  • 1.125 lbs lean ground beef
  • 1 block (14 oz) soft tofu, cubed
  • 3 green onions
  • 4 cloves garlic, 2 tsp ginger
  • 2 tbsp gochujang, 1 tbsp chili garlic
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce, 1/2 cup broth
  • 1 tsp cornstarch (slurry)
  • 3 cups cooked brown rice

Instructions

  1. Sauté garlic, ginger, onion whites.
  2. Add beef and brown.
  3. Stir in gochujang, chili garlic, soy, and broth. Simmer.
  4. Thicken with cornstarch slurry.
  5. Gently fold in soft tofu and onion greens.
  6. Portion over 1 cup rice each.

3. Shrimp & Asparagus Teriyaki

Dinner (4 Servings)

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs large shrimp
  • 2 bunches asparagus, cut
  • 8 oz mushrooms, sliced
  • 4 cups cooked brown rice
  • Sauce:
  • 1/2 cup soy, 1/4 cup water, 2 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar, ginger, garlic
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch

Instructions

  1. Whisk all sauce ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Stir-fry asparagus and mushrooms (5-7 min).
  3. Add shrimp, cook 2-4 min until pink.
  4. Pour in sauce, stir 1-2 min until thick.
  5. Portion over 1 cup rice each.

4. Gingery Tofu & Bok Choy

Dinner (3 Servings)

Ingredients

  • 21 oz extra-firm tofu, cubed
  • 9-10 baby bok choy, halved
  • 6 oz shiitake mushrooms
  • 3 cups cooked brown rice
  • Sauce:
  • 1/4 cup soy, 1/4 cup broth
  • 2 tbsp ginger, 4 cloves garlic
  • 1 tsp sesame oil, 1 tbsp cornstarch

Instructions

  1. Whisk all sauce ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Sauté ginger, garlic, mushrooms, and bok choy stems (3-4 min).
  3. Add bok choy leaves and tofu, stir 1-2 min.
  4. Add sauce, stir gently until thick.
  5. Portion over 1 cup rice each.
